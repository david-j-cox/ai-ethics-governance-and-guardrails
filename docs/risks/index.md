# Risks

Generic "AI in healthcare" risk frameworks underweight the failure modes that show up in specific clinical domains. This section collects domain-specific deep dives.

- [**Domain spotlight: ABA treatment planning**](aba.md): the first deep dive. Aversives in the training corpus, neurodiversity-affirming critique, cultural validity, templating, under-served subpopulations.
- [**Domain spotlight: AI-delivered therapy and mental-health chatbots**](conversational-agents.md): the bounded evidence base for benefit, and the duty-to-warn and crisis-escalation failure modes now documented in litigation.

More domains will be added over time. If you are working in a clinical area not yet covered, the [Foundations](../foundations/index.md), [Architecture](../architecture/index.md), [Evaluation](../evaluation/index.md), and [Governance](../governance/index.md) sections still apply. The risks page is meant to highlight what you'd otherwise miss.
